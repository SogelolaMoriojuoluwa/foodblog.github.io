Health benefits

Learn how lasagne can be a comforting and balanced meal, fitting into a diet that supports a healthier lifestyle.

  • Provides balanced nutrition with a combination of protein, carbohydrates, and fats.
  • Rich in essential vitamins and minerals from ingredients such as meat, cheese, and vegetables, supporting overall health and well-being.
  • High in protein from meat and cheese, essential for muscle growth, repair, and overall body function.
  • Contains fiber if made with whole grain pasta and vegetables, promoting digestive health and regular bowel movements.
